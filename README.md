# Project Overview

- Python for data cleaning, transformation, and feature engineering

# Key Findings
After analyzing customer shopping data, several interesting patterns emerged. Clothing is clearly the star category, bringing in the most revenue, with items like Blouses and Jeans being customer favorites. When it comes to who's spending, Middle-aged and Young Adult customers are the biggest contributors to sales.

We also discovered that only 27% of customers are subscribed, but these subscribers tend to spend differently than non-subscribers. Interestingly, customers who use discounts aren't always bargain hunters ‚Äî many of them still spend above the average amount, showing that discounts can attract quality customers without hurting revenue.

Most products receive solid review ratings around 3.75, and there's a clear group of loyal customers (those with 10+ previous purchases) who keep coming back. Shipping preferences vary, but understanding which types lead to higher purchases can help streamline operations
